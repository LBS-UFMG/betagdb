About


The betagdb is a database with sequences of glucose-tolerant beta-glucosidases. We also performed tridimensional modelage using MODELLER (https://salilab.org/modeller). We also used 3DMOL (http://3dmol.csb.pitt.edu) to show the tridimensional structures.

Cite: Mariano et al. betagdb. Available at: < http://thames.dcc.ufmg.br/betagdb >.
